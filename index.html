How Community Tourism Relay Works

A transparent, community-owned system connecting you directly with local experiences

1

Book Online

Choose your preferred dates and services. Our low-tech relay system ensures your booking reaches the community even without reliable internet.

2

Community Confirmation

Our Tourism Steward receives your booking via SMS and confirms availability with local guides and homestays within hours.

3

Secure Payment

Pay via M-Pesa directly to the community. Your payment is automatically and transparently distributed: 40% guide, 35% homestay, 20% conservancy fund, 5% steward.

About Il Ngwesi

Owned and run by the Maasai community, Il Ngwesi offers something truly special. Visitors to the conservancy have a full wildlife and cultural experience, enjoying hospitality more akin to that offered to a valued family member.

With no other tourist facilities within the Group Ranch, it is the ultimate personal experience. Our Community Tourism Relay system ensures that every booking directly benefits the community while maintaining transparency and accountability.
