Community-Owned Conservation

Il Ngwesi is 100% owned and operated by the Maasai community. With no other tourist facilities in the Group Ranch, visitors enjoy an intimate wildlife and cultural experience with hospitality that feels like family.

Our Community Tourism Relay ensures every booking directly benefits the community while maintaining complete transparency. Tourism revenue supports both wildlife protection and cultural preservation.
